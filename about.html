About KisaanSaathi

KisaanSaathi is a smart agriculture platform built to make farming easier, sustainable, and profitable. Our platform supports farmers with AI tools, real-time information, and local language support.

🌱 Our Mission

Empower every farmer with modern farming technology — regardless of location, education, or internet strength.

  • 🌦 Weather alerts & farming recommendations
  • 🌱 AI-based crop disease detection through photos
  • 💰 Live mandi prices & selling guidance
  • 🗣 Available in regional languages
  • 📶 Works in rural low-internet areas
